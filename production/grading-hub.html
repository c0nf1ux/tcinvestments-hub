Grading Tips & Best Practices

Maximize your grades
Photo Quality
Use high-resolution photos with good lighting. Show all four corners, edges, and surface clearly. Include close-ups of any potential flaws.
Packaging
Use penny sleeves and toploaders. Secure with team bags and bubble wrap. Never use tape directly on the card or holder.
Grade Expectations
Be realistic about condition. Minor flaws can drop grades significantly. Consider professional pre-screening for high-value cards.
ROI Analysis
Factor in grading costs, shipping, and insurance. Not all cards benefit from grading. Use our ROI calculator to make informed decisions.
